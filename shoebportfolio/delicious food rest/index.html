    brief about the Establishment

    Delicious Food Restauarant is an Indian chain of western food restaurants headquartered in Noida.The chain specializes in europian,italian and french food and cuisines. It was established in 2021 by JSS Academy Of Technical Education Noida student Shoeb Ahmad Khan. As of December 2021, there are around 5 outlets across 3 cities in India. The company aims to be an IPO in a few years with plans to gain a global foothold.

    The space is designed to cater a casual catch-up over artisanal coffee, small bites, large plates, baked goods, tea blends, elegant cocktails, generous pours and hearty portions.
